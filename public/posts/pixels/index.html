Trying some pixel art

June 20, 2026

I’m not sure I like pixel art#

Or rather, I like looking at it, but find it infuriating to make. I’ll keep trying to see if I can get more into it, but for now I’ve made a little campfire for the home page. I’m quite proud of it, all things considered. I can’t imagine making a whole website of pixel art, I’d legit loose it.

I also found a neat CRT effect on this site. I’m going to try and apply to the campfire and see if it’s better looking?
