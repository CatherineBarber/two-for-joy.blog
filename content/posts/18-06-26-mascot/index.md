I started sketching a mascot / logo kind of thing. There's lots of magpues in my area so there's no shortage of reference photos. This cutie has a little white dot next to their eye.
![Venn Dia-agram](venn.png)
I've kept the white dot and used the pics I took as a ref for the pose. I thought it would be neat if I could generally fit them into a sort of square shape? Like it might make it easier to create a logo if the dimensions are regular. It almost works, and if you fill the top left corner with an icon it fits nicely (moon / start / sun / 'TFJ' could all work).
